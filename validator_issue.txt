- total_checks = self.checks_performed (incremented during validation)
- passed_checks = self.checks_passed (incremented when check passes)
- failed_checks = total_checks - passed_checks
- completion_percentage = (checks_passed / checks_performed) × 100
  In your case: (22 ÷ 26) × 100 = 84.62% ✓
- compliance_percentage = 100 - (error_count × 5) (from config)
  In your case: 100 - (1 × 5) = 95% ✓
- error_count = Count of actual errors found
- warning_count = Count of actual warnings found
